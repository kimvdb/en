 Glass

Ameode

Year 2014-2017

Within this project her work focuses on the research of shapes, textures and the tension of a shape. It consists of organic shapes with tauted contours. She gets her inspiration from the outlines of interesting human postures.

Then she makes a 2D design of it and from there on she makes a primary study in 3D. These are the foundations for the work, but she doesn't fixes the form of it. The bodies grow and rise from the material itself. This is achieved by continuously adjusting the form through formation. She wants to play with coincidences in a process where a lot of control is needed. 


These are one by one abstract bodies which she eventually cuts through to open up the work's surface, in order to create a contrast between the cut and the body. Here she can play along with the control of the cuts, by being relentless or cautious.
